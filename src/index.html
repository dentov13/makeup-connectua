Common vision of future

The idea behind the Connect Ukraine fest is to construct a creative playground for 2000 innovative people, to inspire and motivate them to change the world, making their vision the reality. To accomplish that, the project will also provide them with tools, skills and resources. The festival is meant to become a meeting point for all the innovators and investors and bring fresh ideas and possibilities, sort of a market showcase of the best of what Ukrainian creative industry has to offer.

There are not much things as exciting as the process of creation—unless, co-creation. On July 28-30 more than 2000 social entrepreneurs and corporates, visionary innovators and creative artists, technologists and ethical hackers from Ukraine and all over world will converge in Kiev to brainstorm ideas, develop prototypes and co-create projects that will redefine the future of Ukraine.

Over three days filled with theory and practice, participants—with the help of corporate sponsors and non-profit organizations—will learn how to define the unique purpose of their life and projects, and inscribe their individual ideas into a greater vision of the country’s development. They will explore how to take action, will develop prototypes that can be tested and realized in real world.

The mission of the project is to spark innovation and creativity within Ukraine, to boost the creative sector of national economics, re-evaluate its underrated creative assets and make it attractive to inner and international investment.
The format of the festival is day conferences + night entertainment and networking. The day program consists of 50% inspiring talks and 50% workshops.

In the mornings we will have lectures from some of the world’s most disruptive innovators, visionary creatives and social entrepreneurs; topics will include virtual and augmented reality, AI and automation, social impact and sustainability, bitcoin and blockchain.

In the afternoons they will give workshops based on design thinking, ideation, prototyping, and how technologies of launching a real social impact business, meant to give practical skills in creation, collaboration and entrepreneurship.
The Festival is being organized by an international team of passionaries having the unshakable faith in the future of Ukraine, top-notch pros working in such fields as social entrepreneurship, co-creation, events, media, design, music, IT and game design, media and digital, NGO sector, startups and investment.

We are proud to call our partners Art Zavod Platforma, Startup Ukraine, Made In Ukraine, Raiffeisen Bank, Bitcoin Nation, Emerging Future Institute, Eurotrips, Katapult Future Fest (Norway), DNB (Norway), Future Leaders (Norway),

The Project’s Co-Founders are Norwegian social entrepreneur Jorgen Bo and Sophia Yushchenko, the daughter of former Ukrainian President.

APP

All the participants of the Festival have the opportunity to present their own ideas and projects and have them analyzed, discussed, questioned, and improved. The expected outcome of the festival is 500 projects, that are ready to be carried out.

Download the APP and you will be able to select your field of interest, register your project, get access to the collective creative body, and receive professional feedback. It will allow you to be updated about the daily schedule, the most interesting discussions and improvised networking sessions. Along with that it will enable you to stay connected with people you meet—speakers, experts, colleagues, potential partners and investors, making you a part of the Ukrainian creative network.

The APP is available after the ticket purchase.




LOCATION
The festival will be held at the Art Zavod Platforma, a well-known creative cluster of the Kiev City, that presents and hosts the most creative and innovative events. Beautifully designed 120000 square meters of Art Zav od will provide participants with leisurely shadow and grass for chats and workshops during the day, and with stages and dancefloors at night.
